Platform Features

Everything you need to
own your audience

🎬

Multi-Provider Streaming

Connect Mux, Vimeo, Brightcove, or JW Player. Videos stay with your provider — VIBE controls the access layer with signed playback tokens.

💳

Stripe Connect Payouts

Creators connect their Stripe Express account and get paid automatically. Your platform fee is split at checkout — no manual invoicing.

🏆

Certificate Engine

System-verified completion tracking triggers eligibility. Admins approve and issue branded certificates. Perfect for training businesses.

🔑

Entitlement Control

Every playback request checks a live entitlement — not just a subscription status. Library access, course access, or time-limited rentals.

🎨

Full White-Label

Each creator gets their own logo, colors, and custom domain. Your learners never see VIBE branding unless you want them to.

📊

Creator Analytics

Revenue tracking, learner progress, course completion rates, and certificate pipeline — all in one admin dashboard per tenant.
